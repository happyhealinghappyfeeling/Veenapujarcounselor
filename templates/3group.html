Group Counselling

Support groups for survivors of narcissitic abuse are highly beneficial


They provide a safe, non judgemental space to share experiences with others who understand. Groups helps to challenge the reality distortion caused by gaslighting & manipulation, helping survivors regain clarity & self turst. they offer a corrective emotional experience, helping rebuild a sense of belongingness & validation that may be missing from the original relationship.

Group counselling sessions include a skills based approaches like:

Reflective leasoning - Group cohession

Catharsis - provides a safe place for members to express emotion & feel a sense of release & relief.

Universality - The reliazation that one is not alone in their struggles.
