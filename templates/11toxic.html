How To Deal With Toxic People

Set clear boundaries


At workplace you may be working under toxic boss who constantly put you under pressure & even he can try to create negative environment in the team by manipulation or hostility.

Focus on your wellbeing communicate assertively & minimize contact, avoid getting drawn into their negativity & disengage from unproductive arguments by calmly stating your needs with 'I' statements. If necessary distance yourself or in extreme cases seek help from counselling psychologist/therapist like me as toxic people can't always be fixed.


The key strategies to deal with toxic people :

Set clear boundaries about the unaccecptable behavior.

Focus on solution instead of engaging in drama.

Limit interactions when possible.

Don't respond to provocation publicly.

Seek support from co-workers or superviser or HR who can help manage the situation effectively.
